About Me

My name is Christopher Sayles. I am very excited to begin a long and prosperous programming career. My past work experience include roles as a senior software development analyst and a project managment business analyst for a large global consulting firm, where I traveled to Philadelphia bi-weekly. Before consulting I worked as a configuration specialist for a healthcare provider in Austin, TX. I graduated with a degree in marketing from Western Michigan University.

In my free time I love watching sports. My favorite teams are the Michigan Wolverines, Detroit Tigers, and Detroit Pistons. I enjoy learning new songs on guitar and piano. In my spare time I can also be found playing video games with friends. I love to travel to foreign countries.
